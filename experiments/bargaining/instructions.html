Instructions

You will be paired randomly and anonymously with another participant. In this study, one of you will be Participant 1 (Buyer) and the other Participant 2 (Seller). Prior to making a decision, you will learn your role, which will be randomly assigned.

In each buyer sends a message to a seler, making an offer for the bicycle. Buyer's willingness to pay of bicycle is 100. The seller can either accept or reject the buyer's offer, but does not have an opportunity to make a counteroffer. If the buyer offers a price of P, the buyer gets profits of 100-p and the seller gets profits of p. If the seller rejects the offer, the bike is abandoned and buyer and seller both get profits of 0.
